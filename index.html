About the robot

robocode-kkc-pikachu is a Robocode robot implementing the following strategy

Movement: Pikachu robot tracks the energy level of the enemy and when there is a small drop in energy it assumes the enemy just fired a bullet so it changes direction.

Targeting: Pikachu uses its radar to scan for the enemy robot. It continually oscillates the radar so it can continually track the enemy.

Firing Pikachu uses its radar to calculate where the enemy is and aims its gun at the enemy. Pikachu then fires a bullet at the enemy with power based on the distance of the enemy. The further the enemy from Pikachu, the weaker the power of the bullet fired.
